About

Bachelor of Science in Informatics with a specialization in Human Computer Interaction from the Donald Bren School of Information & Computer Science at the University of California, Irvine.

Interested in designing and developing web-based tools that provide organizations with an automated method for processing, accessing and visualizing data. Designing mobile solutions, creation of concepts, wireframes, flowcharts, prototypes, and detailed mockups as well as conduct user testing and document project requirements.

My digital art background has helped me deliver creative interface designs and original project team logos for school and internship projects. I love Informatics because I’ve learned how to design better user interfaces by studying and observing how people use information technology. I've also researched how organizations use information technology to support their practices focused on helping users by providing them with efficient innovative interfaces.
